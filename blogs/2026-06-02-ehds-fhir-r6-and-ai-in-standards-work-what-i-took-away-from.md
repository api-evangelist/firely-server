At the HL7 Working Group Meeting in Rotterdam, EU countries reported varying progress on EHDS implementation, with most determining realistic data offerings for 2029. FHIR R6 continues advancing toward normative status, and HL7 ran successful experiments using AI to analyze specification issues and identify internal inconsistencies across thousands of pages.
